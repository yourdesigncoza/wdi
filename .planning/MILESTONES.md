# Milestones: WillCraft SA

## v1.0 MVP (Shipped: 2026-02-08)

**Delivered:** Complete AI-powered will generation platform for South Africa — from POPIA consent through conversational AI data collection, complex estate handling, dual-LLM verification, PDF generation, PayFast payment, post-purchase updates, and supplementary documents.

**Phases completed:** 01-09 (43 plans total across 10 phases)

**Key accomplishments:**

- POPIA-compliant infrastructure with UPL prevention and attorney-approved clause library architecture
- Clerk authentication with JWT dual-gate enforcement (POPIA + auth)
- AI-guided conversational will creation with OpenAI GPT-4o-mini and SSE streaming
- Complex estate scenarios: blended families, testamentary trusts, usufruct, business assets, joint wills
- Dual-LLM verification (Gemini checks OpenAI) with SA Wills Act rules validation
- Professional PDF generation via WeasyPrint with witness instructions and disclaimers
- PayFast payment integration with ITN webhooks, secure download tokens, and email backup
- Save/resume and unlimited post-purchase will updates with version tracking
- Living will and funeral wishes supplementary document generation
